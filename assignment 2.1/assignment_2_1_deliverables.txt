Assignment 2.1 Deliverables: Reflective Customer Feedback Agent
=================================================================

Objective
---------
Build an AI agent that critiques and refines its analysis of customer feedback iteratively using LangChain and OpenAI. The agent applies the Reflection Pattern by producing an initial analysis, refining that analysis, and repeating the process over multiple iterations.

1. Python Script
----------------
The reflective agent implementation is contained in the following project files:

- src/feedback_reflection/agent.py
- src/feedback_reflection/main.py

agent.py defines the ReflectiveFeedbackAgent class, including the LangChain prompts used to analyze and refine customer feedback. main.py provides the sample customer feedback, runs the agent, and prints each iteration of the reflection process.

The script analyzes customer feedback for positive, negative, and neutral sentiment. It then suggests business improvements and refines those suggestions over multiple iterations.

4. Reflection Report
--------------------
The agent used an iterative reflection process to improve its analysis of customer feedback. In the first stage, it identified the feedback as mixed because the customer experienced late delivery and damaged packaging, but also praised the customer service team for being helpful.

Across the refinement cycles, the analysis became clearer and more useful. The suggestions moved from broad recommendations, such as improving delivery and packaging, toward more practical business actions. These included improving delivery reliability, strengthening packaging quality control, maintaining strong customer service practices, and considering customer retention actions for customers who have had a poor experience.

The reflection process was useful because it encouraged the agent to review and improve its own previous response. Instead of stopping at a basic sentiment summary, the agent produced a more detailed and actionable final analysis. This showed how the Reflection Pattern can improve the quality of text analysis by adding review, refinement, and prioritization steps.

5. Reflection Questions
-----------------------

Question 1: How did the agent's suggestions evolve over iterations?

The agent's suggestions became more specific, structured, and actionable over the iterations. Early suggestions focused on the obvious issues in the feedback, such as late delivery and damaged packaging. Later refinements improved the business value of the recommendations by suggesting clearer operational actions, such as improving delivery processes, strengthening packaging checks, and preserving the positive customer service experience.

Question 2: What aspects of the Reflection Pattern were most effective?

The refinement step was the most effective part of the Reflection Pattern. It allowed the agent to reconsider its initial analysis and make the response clearer, deeper, and more feasible. This helped turn a basic sentiment analysis into a more useful set of recommendations for the business.

Question 3: How can this approach be applied to other text analysis tasks?

This approach can be applied to many other text analysis tasks, including product reviews, support tickets, employee surveys, complaint analysis, social media comments, and customer satisfaction surveys. In each case, the agent can create an initial analysis, reflect on it, refine its insights, and produce a more polished final response or action plan.
